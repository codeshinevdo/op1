Who We Are

Born From Empathy, Built on Action

Anandham Youth Foundation began in 2012, born from a team of friends from humble rural backgrounds who found success in the city — and turned back to lift others. We felt the pain of over 2 lakh students discontinuing education due to poverty in Tamil Nadu.

Today we provide 100% financial support to brilliant, underprivileged students pursuing MBBS, Engineering, Arts & Science across Tamil Nadu's most prestigious institutions.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Belief

Our Mission

Provide higher education to underprivileged, deserving students, enriched with life skills and value-based learning — empowering them to face challenges with confidence.

Our Vision

Empowering every youth to reach their full potential, grow consistently, and live happily.

Our Belief

Life values are more important than formal education. For us, it's not about producing graduates — but shaping confident individuals who lead a happy life.

Home › The Inspiring Journey

Our History

Fueled by Passion, Driven by Purpose

A team of friends from humble backgrounds, who made it in the city and decided to give back — this is our journey.

2002
The Seeds Are Planted
A group of friends begin conducting life skills and value-based educational programs, reaching out to 70,000+ students in schools and colleges across Tamil Nadu.
2012
Anandham Youth Foundation is Born
Realizing over 2 lakh students discontinue education due to poverty, the team formalizes the foundation. Registered under Trusts Act with Reg. No: 452/12.
2015
First 100 Students Supported
The foundation crosses a landmark milestone — 100 students supported in MBBS, Engineering, and Arts & Science programs at prestigious institutions.
2020
APJ Abdul Kalam Award
Founder Selvakumar receives the Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Award — the highest recognition by the Tamil Nadu State Government for contributions in humanities and student welfare.
2024
980+ Students & Growing
The foundation now supports hundreds of students annually, with a strong alumni network, placement program, and girl student welfare initiative making a lasting impact.
2026 & Beyond
The Journey Continues
With new CSR partnerships, digital expansion, and the Student Application 2026 open — the foundation is growing stronger, reaching further, and dreaming bigger.
